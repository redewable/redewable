Financing Considerations

Phase I Refresh Requirement

Equity backers, financiers, and investment banks typically consider Phase I reports valid for 6 months. After that threshold, they require a refresh—even if nothing has changed. For a develop-and-sell strategy, Phase I timing should align with anticipated buyer due diligence window. Desktop studies and CIA can be completed earlier; the full Phase I ESA should be timed to remain current through the sale process.
