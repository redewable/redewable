Firm Power at Scale

A cleared 600 MW interconnection in West Texas, configured for firm power and built to your phase plan.

ReDew Anson controls a cleared 600 MW envelope at the in-service Phantom Hill 345 kV substation — studied to 2 GW under normal conditions — with site control established across 168 acres. We're developing it as an AI-grade data center and long-duration energy campus, configured as a Large-Duty Uninterruptible Power System (LDUPS): battery in-series between the grid and your load, with on-site generation funded, owned, and operated by our power-generation partner and passed through to you at cost on a triple-net basis. Phase 1 delivers 74.5 MW of billable critical IT on a modular build; three 200 MW phases follow on your roadmap.

Phase 1 files as a small-load application directly with Lone Star Transmission — outside the 226 GW ERCOT large-load queue. Studies run roughly nine months to a signed position in H1 2027, against the five-to-seven years a new large-load interconnection commonly takes.

Configuration

LDUPS — battery in-series between the grid and your load.

The campus is configured as a Large-Duty Uninterruptible Power System. Long-duration vanadium flow storage sits in-series between the substation and the data center load; IPP-owned on-site generation feeds the load bus in parallel. From your perspective, the data center sees firm, conditioned power independent of grid state — the battery and the gas plant carry through grid events, and the substation provides primary supply.

1.
Firm, conditioned power Battery and on-site gas together deliver tenant-grade uptime independent of grid state.
2.
Grid-independent ride-through LDUPS handles grid events without dropping load. The substation is supply, not single point.
3.
Capacity that earns its keep Storage simultaneously serves ERCOT arbitrage and ancillaries — lower effective tenant cost-of-power than firming alone.

Interconnection engineering by EPE (Electric Power Engineers), working directly with Lone Star Transmission, the transmission service provider at Phantom Hill. Specialized regulatory counsel is engaged for an independent legal opinion on the configuration as part of tenant diligence.
