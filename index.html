Why It Matters

An asset to the grid. A win for Texas.

Texans remember what happens when the grid runs thin. We're building storage that helps make sure it doesn't — sited where the grid needs it, on a timeline the grid can use, with technology that's safe for the community and built at home.

Path to Power
A clear route to interconnection.

Land secured. Environmental complete. Interconnection underway at Phantom Hill 345 kV — one of the most strategic nodes in ERCOT West.

Time to Power
Ready when Texas needs it.

On track for mid-2027 NTP and commercial operations 18 months later. Pre-development work done up front means power on the grid when demand is rising fastest.

An Asset, Not a Liability
Storage that firms the grid.

Dispatchable capacity that reduces curtailment, supports reliability, and pays its way — not another draw on the system, but a stabilizer for it.

Next-Generation Tech
Long-duration. Vanadium-backed.

Vanadium flow chemistry. 100% domestically sourced. 100% recyclable. No water waste. No fire risk. Storage Texas can be proud of and live next to.

The Land · Jones County, Texas

Putting tired land back to work.

The parcel is dryland farmland that has run at roughly a quarter of its historical yield for more than a decade — the water table has dropped, the rain has shifted, and the economics of cropping it have gone with them. Right next door sits the Phantom Hill 345 kV substation. The same ground that won't support a profitable harvest will support the grid Texas actually needs. And the facility we're putting on it is designed to belong here.

How We Work

We don’t build these alone. We bring the right people together.

The hardest part of a project this size isn’t the technology — it’s orchestration. Knowing which specialists a project needs, and bringing them in at the right moment, in the right order. We’re the least technical people in the room, by design: our job is to convene the team, not to be it.

Assemble the Pieces

Every project is a puzzle — land, capital, technology, interconnection, offtake, permitting, community. We hold the whole picture and fit the pieces together in the sequence that gets it built.

Make the Introductions

Our edge isn’t being the expert — it’s knowing who is. We bring in the best in every seat and connect them precisely when it moves a project forward.

Give First

Go-giver, not go-getter. We bring value to every table before we ask for any. Generosity offered up front, and trust earned over time, compound — and they make stronger projects than transactions ever will.
