Who We Are

About ReDewable

ReDewable is a development-stage BESS company based in College Station, TX. Our model is develop-to-sell — we source, site, and de-risk utility-scale battery storage projects through all pre-development milestones, then sell to a strategic buyer who constructs and operates. We don't intend to become an IPP. We build shovel-ready assets for partners who do.

Project Thesis

Why This Site

Grid Demand
2.5+ GW of utility-scale solar operational or queued in Jones County creates direct storage demand at this node. Adjacent to ENGIE's ~620 MW solar installation.
Cleared Feasibility
600 MW confirmed at 345 kV with no local thermal constraints — a hard-to-replicate grid position in ERCOT WEST.
Stargate Proximity
19 miles north of the Stargate AI data center campus in Abilene. AI-driven power demand in this corridor is a core thesis for the project's long-term revenue profile.
