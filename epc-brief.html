EPC Engagement
What We're Seeking
Campus Construction
A construction partner for sitework and civil, substation and grid tie, gas lateral, water infrastructure, and campus integration. Battery is turnkey under TerraFlow; the data center is modular / prefab; the gas plant is built and owned by the IPP.
Schedule Discipline
Phase 1 targets mid-2028 energization on a 6–12 month modular build. Construction ramp begins as soon as the signed position is in hand — partner needs to plan resourcing against a defined mid-2027 NTP.
Tenant-Grade Execution
An anchor data center tenant will hold construction to hyperscale standards on quality, schedule, and commissioning. Partners with comparable build history in ERCOT preferred.
Project Thesis
Why Now · Why Here
Cleared MW at Scale
600 MW confirmed at Phantom Hill 345 kV with no local thermal constraints. A queue-cleared position the system can no longer easily reproduce.
Tenant Demand
AI compute build-out has shifted the binding constraint to firm power at scale. The Stargate AI campus near Abilene sits ~19 miles south of the site.
Phased Energization
Phase 1 plus three 200 MW phases deployed inside the cleared envelope. Signed position H1 2027, NTP mid-2027, Phase 1 energized mid-2028.
