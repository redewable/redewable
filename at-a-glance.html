Why This Deal

Derisked Development

300 MW independently confirmed feasible at 345 kV. Land secured on long-term lease. Environmental desktop clean. SLD complete. ERCOT application in progress. Financing structured for transferability at closing. The hardest development risks — injection capacity, land, permitting pathway — are already retired.

Structural Market Advantage

West Texas curtailment exceeds 5 TWh/yr and growing. 600+ MW storage deficit in Jones County. ERCOT demand up 60% by 2030 while transmission relief is $33B and years away. This location captures congestion-driven arbitrage that fleet-average BESS economics miss entirely. RTC+B reform (live Dec 2025) further rewards sophisticated dispatch.

Below-Market Capital Stack

All-in installed cost of ~$490–$520/kW vs. NREL benchmark of $480–$560/kW for competitive 4hr BESS. Turnkey EPC at ~$440/kW ($132M) from Optimum with CATL + SMA. No land acquisition cost — 20–30 year lease eliminates a major capital line item. Development premium of $50–$80/kW ($15M–$24M) reflects retired risk, not speculative value.

Clean Acquisition Path

Milestone-based structure available. Financing structured to convey at closing — non-accelerating, no prepayment penalty. Full operational flexibility with no dispatch or offtake restrictions. Buyer-aligned OEM and inverter already specified. Full market data and capital stack detail provided for buyer-led returns modeling.

ERCOT Market Thesis

West Zone: Highest Curtailment in ERCOT

Transmission congestion creates a structural arbitrage opportunity. Surplus renewable generation can't reach eastern demand centers — BESS captures stranded value at the source.

BTU Analytics / FactSet, ERCOT (Feb 2025)  |  Full Report →

Demand Growth

ERCOT is the fastest-growing grid in the US. Peak demand projected to reach 138 GW by 2030 — up 60% from 87 GW in 2025. Data center interconnection requests tracking 3x above 2024.

Transmission Relief: Years Away

Permian Basin Reliability Plan: 765 kV import paths — ERCOT's first extra-high voltage lines. Estimated $33B total cost. BESS bridges the gap until infrastructure arrives.

Co-Location Revenue

H1 2025: co-located solar captured 72% of locational price vs. 57% standalone — 15pp advantage. BESS shifts solar past sunset into peak pricing.

SB-6 Compliance Tailwind

Senate Bill 6 (2026) requires facilities >75 MW to curtail during grid emergencies. Behind-the-meter BESS enables data center compliance while maintaining uptime.
