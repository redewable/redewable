04 / Inflection

The buyer who values this asset most isn't optimizing for 2-hour merchant arbitrage.

What changed

As we watched the buyer set evolve — hyperscalers chasing 24/7 clean firm power, the Stargate buildout, West Zone curtailment growing every quarter — it became clear which buyer values this asset most. It's the one who needs firmed renewables on a 24/7 profile, built domestically, designed to last 20+ years.

Two-hour Li-ion was never going to be the answer for that buyer. Long-duration is.

What it takes

We have the land. We have the feasibility. We have the environmental and the permitting. A long-duration OEM brings the technology, the equipment, and the manufacturing capability.

The final piece of the puzzle is a customer who finds value in firmed capacity and a contracted rate over a long horizon. That customer exists. They're already in our corridor.
