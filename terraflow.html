01 / Foundation

A Texas-based developer building grid infrastructure for the buyers who are showing up.

ReDew develops shovel-ready storage assets at strategically-located interconnection points. We focus on the work that comes before construction: land, environmental, interconnection, and the partnership architecture that turns a site into a financeable project.

The next era of the grid has to be domestically built, long-duration, and designed for the buyers who are actually showing up — hyperscalers, industrials, and a system stressed in ways it has never been before. That conviction shapes every project we touch.

The corridor — Phantom Hill 345 kV at the center of an unprecedented concentration of solar generation and hyperscaler load.

This is the largest data center buildout in history, and it is happening in our corridor. The Stargate clean energy campus is underway. Vantage Data Centers' Frontier campus in Shackelford County is underway. Many gigawatts of load are arriving inside three years.

Meanwhile, the West Load Zone is curtailing solar at a rate that grows every quarter. The infrastructure that exists wasn't designed for what's coming. The interconnection points that can host the answer are scarce — and we have one of them.
