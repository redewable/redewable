The Asset

Cleared 600 MW at a 345 kV node, ~19 miles from the largest AI compute build in North America.

ReDew Anson holds 600 MW of cleared injection capacity at Phantom Hill 345 kV — a queue-cleared interconnection at scale that the AI compute market needs and the queue can no longer easily reproduce. We're developing it as a data center, on-site natural gas, and long-duration vanadium flow storage campus, configured as a Large-Duty UPS (battery in-series between grid and load), held and operated behind an anchor data center tenant.

Capacity is the bottleneck. We control 600 MW of it, next to the corridor that needs it most.

Investment Thesis

Four facts the bet rests on.

i.
Cleared MW is the moat.
New 600 MW positions at 345 kV nodes are not being created in the ERCOT queue at the pace AI demand is consuming them. Cleared capacity at scale is non-fungible — you cannot buy your way back to one with money in the queue.
ii.
AI compute has set the clock.
The Stargate AI campus near Abilene sits ~19 miles south. Hyperscalers and AI infrastructure operators are reserving capacity today for energization windows two-plus years out. The buyer is already in market.
iii.
LDUPS stacks three revenue streams.
Battery in-series captures ERCOT arbitrage, sells ancillary services, and firms the tenant's power — from one asset position. Hold-and-operate lets the operator capture all three. Develop-and-sell would surrender most of it.
iv.
Phased monetization derisks the bet.
25–50 MW tranches inside the cleared envelope are independently financeable, fillable, and monetizable. First-tranche economics close before full-envelope COD. No single-build cliff.

Structure

Block 1 SPV. Capital back first. Pro-rata into the envelope.

i.
Block 1 SPV vehicle
ReDew Anson Block 1, LLC — a single-purpose vehicle holding the first 50 MW tranche. ReDewable Energy Company serves as Managing Member with full operational control; investors hold passive membership units.
ii.
Capital-back-first waterfall
Invested capital is returned in full at the first-block refinance, ahead of any distributions. Pro-rata distributions follow on a defined cadence post-COD.
iii.
Pro-rata into Blocks 2–12
Block 1 investors hold first-right participation as subsequent tranches capitalize across the cleared 600 MW envelope. Non-equity financing (ITC monetization, C-PACE, project debt) enters as debt, not equity — preserving the equity stack.

Reg D Rule 506(b), accredited investors only. Equity stake, minimum investment, governance protections, and the full term sheet detail in the data room. Terms pending counsel review.
