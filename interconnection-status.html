Current Status

Feasibility complete. GINR in preparation. IA in 2027.

ReDew Anson holds 600 MW of cleared injection capacity at Phantom Hill 345 kV. Independent feasibility studies are complete. The GINR application package is in preparation under TerraFlow, with a co-located LDUPS configuration (battery in-series between grid and data center load). IA target mid-2027; NTP follows; first tranche 50 MW energized 2028. GINR has not yet been submitted.

GINR Package

In preparation — co-located LDUPS configuration.

The GINR application package is being prepared under TerraFlow's lead. Components: single-line diagram, PSS/E and PSCAD models, reactive power study, MQT compliance, and RARF/RIOO form. The configuration submitted with the package is co-located LDUPS — battery in-series between grid and data center load — supported by deep ERCOT, Centerpoint, and Oncor relationships through our technology partner. Specialized ERCOT/FERC counsel will provide an independent legal opinion on the configuration as part of tenant and capital diligence.
